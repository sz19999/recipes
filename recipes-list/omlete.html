A Regular Omelette

Description

I'm sure you know how to make an Omelette, perhaps better than mine even. However, maybe you forgot how to make one. So here's a quick recipe on how to make an easy, tasty Omelette in 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Eggs
  • Salt (Optional: Other spices, e.g. Pepper)
  • Milk (Optional)

Steps

  1. Grab 2/3 eggs, Break them in a bowl of your choice.
  2. Add some spices you like, I usually add some salt and pepper.
  3. Add a little milk, if you wish. It gives a puffiness.
  4. Whisk everything with a fork until it has a uniform clean look.
  5. Put a pan on the stove, let it sit and warm up a little, and then pour the mixture.
  6. Let it cook a bit, then flip it. Cook it until it becomes solid, but don't overcook it. If you do, it becomes too dry.
  7. That's it, you did it! Now throw your masterpiece Omelette on a plate. Add some leaves above, perhaps cut some vegetables and put it aside. Feel free to use your imagination, have fun! and of course bon appetite!
