A Regular Omlete

Description

I'm sure you know how to make an Omlete, perhaps better than mine even. However, maybe you forgot how to make one. So here's a quick recipe on how to make an easy, perhaps tasty Omlete in 5 minutes.

  1. Grab 2/3 eggs, Break them in a bowl of your choice.
  2. Add some spices you like, I usually add some salt and pepper.
  3. Add a little milk, if you wish. It gives a puffiness.
  4. Whisk everything with a fork untill it has a uniform clean look.
  5. Put a pan on the stove, let it sit and warm up a little, and then pour the mixture.
  6. Let it cook a bit, then flip it. Cook it untill it becomes solid, but dont overcook it. If you do, it becomes too dry.
  7. That's it, you did it! Now throw the your masterpiece Omlete on a plate. Add some leaves above, perhaps cut some vegtables and put it aside. Feel free to use your imagination, have fun! and ofcourse bon apetite!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Eggs
  • Salt (Optional: Other spices, e.g. Pepper)
  • Milk (Optional)
